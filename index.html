Work

As a passionate software developer, I have been working on several projects that showcase my skills and creativity.

One of my most recent projects involved creating a bot to automate the access of university students on Discord using Python. This bot was designed to make it easier for students to join the relevant university channels on Discord without having to navigate through multiple menus. It was a challenging project, but I learned a lot about Python and automation techniques using Pandas.

Another project I am particularly proud of is the creation of a full 3D world in a website using JavaScript. It allowed me to showcase my skills in web development and graphic design. The website was designed to be visually appealing and user-friendly, and it was well-received by my peers.

I am also one of the creators of an algorithm that attempts to predict Parkinson's disease. This was a particularly meaningful project for me, as it involved using my programming skills to help solve a real-world problem. I was able to collaborate with other experts in the field, and I am proud of the impact that our algorithm could have on diagnosing Parkinson's disease.

In addition to these projects, I also manage online groups with almost 6000 people. This involves coordinating events and activities and ensuring that everyone is on the same page.

Finally, I also enjoy editing photos and videos using Adobe programs. This is a passion of mine, and it allows me to express my creativity in a different way. I take pride in the work that I do, and I am always looking for new opportunities to showcase my skills.
